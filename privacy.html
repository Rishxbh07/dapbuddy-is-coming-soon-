Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2025

This Privacy Policy (“Policy”) explains how DapBuddy Technologies, a sole proprietorship established in Maharashtra, India (“Company”, “we”, “our”, “us”), collects, stores, processes, uses, discloses, and protects Personal Information when you (“User”, “you”, “your”) access or use the DapBuddy mobile application, website, or related features (“Platform”). By accessing or using the Platform, you acknowledge and accept this Policy.

1. Data Controller & Contact

DapBuddy Technologies, Maharashtra, India
Email (primary contact & grievance officer): support@dapbuddy.com

2. Applicability & Scope

This Policy applies to Users (Members), Hosts, prospective users, and visitors of the Platform. It covers data collected directly, automatically, or through authorized third parties. The Platform is intended only for users in India.

3. Definitions

  • Personal Information: Data that identifies or can reasonably identify an individual (e.g., name, email, phone).
  • Sensitive Personal Data: Passwords, authentication credentials, financial information.
  • Service Credentials: Login data or access tokens for third-party subscription services (e.g., Netflix, Spotify).
  • Logs: Verification logs, access proof, Host delivery timestamps, and audit records.
  • Joining Fee: Fee consisting of Base Price + Platform Fee + Access Protection Fee + taxes.

4. Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to operate and secure the Platform.

4.1 Information You Provide

  • Name, email, phone
  • Account password (hashed/encrypted, never stored plaintext)
  • Profile info you voluntarily provide
  • Dispute-related evidence (screenshots, emails)

4.2 Payment Metadata

We collect: UPI handle (VPA), Transaction IDs, Payment timestamps, and Gateway confirmations. We do NOT store: Card numbers, CVV, UPI PIN, OTP, or Banking passwords. All financial processing is done by licensed payment gateways.

4.3 Subscription Credentials

If required for a shared subscription, credentials may be temporarily processed and retained only as needed. They are deleted within 24 hours after tenure ends, unless a dispute exists. If a dispute exists, logs & related data are retained for up to 50 days.

4.4 Device & Behavioural Data

We may collect IP address (approx.), Device model, OS, Browser/app version, Usage logs, and Fraud & security behavioural signals.

5. Purpose of Data Use

We use Personal Information for:

  • Account creation and security
  • Matchmaking between Hosts & Members based on approximate location
  • Facilitating payments, renewals, and Joining Fees
  • Fraud detection, dispute resolution, abuse prevention
  • Platform improvement and debugging
  • Legal compliance & audit requirements

6. Legal Basis

Based on Indian law, we process data under: Consent, Contractual necessity, Legitimate interests (fraud prevention, security), and Compliance with legal obligations.

7. Sharing of Data

We may share limited information with payment gateways, cloud infrastructure providers, customer support & moderation staff, and regulatory or law enforcement bodies (where required). We do NOT sell personal information, share with advertisers, or monetize user data.

8. Data Security

We use TLS/SSL encryption, encrypted storage of sensitive elements, hashed passwords, role-based staff access, verification logs instead of storing passwords, and secure deletion mechanisms.

9. Retention Policy

  • Account data: kept until deletion or legal requirement
  • Credentials: deleted after tenure + 24 hours, unless dispute
  • Logs for disputes: retained up to 50 days
  • Payment metadata: retained for compliance & reconciliation

10. Data Breach & Liability

If a breach occurs, we will notify affected users and authorities as required and investigate the incident. We are not liable for breaches caused by third-party attacks, Host negligence (e.g., weak passwords), or events outside our control. We do not store sensitive payment data.

11. Cookies & Tracking

We use essential cookies (login, session) and analytics cookies (performance improvement). We do not use advertising cookies.

12. Children

Users under 18 may use the Platform only with parental/guardian consent. Parents/guardians are legally responsible for all actions of minors. Hosts must be 18+.

13. Third-Party Services

We are not responsible for privacy practices of streaming platforms, payment gateways, or cloud providers. Links are provided for convenience only.

14. User Rights

You may request access, correction, deletion, or withdrawal of consent. These requests may be denied if ongoing investigations, security issues, or legal obligations apply.

15. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy at any time. Material updates will be notified via email or Platform notice.
